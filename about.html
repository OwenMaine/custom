Forged in Miami's Streets

Custom Speed Creations wasn't born in a boardroom; it was conceived in the late-night hum of a Cutler Bay garage. We are a collective of engineers, artists, and gearheads united by a single obsession: pushing the limits of automotive style and performance. We believe a car should be more than a machine—it should be a statement, an extension of your personality.

Our Journey

2015: The Beginning

Our founder, a veteran performance tuner, opens a small garage in Cutler Bay, focusing on engine swaps and custom ECU maps for the local street scene.

2018: First Major Build

Completed our first full-scale, award-winning build—a widebody import that gained national attention and put CSC on the map.

2021: Shop Expansion

Moved into our current 10,000 sq. ft. facility, adding a dedicated paint booth and state-of-the-art fabrication equipment.

Today: Pushing Boundaries

Recognized as one of South Florida's elite shops for one-of-a-kind vehicle personalization, from hypercars to modern classics.

Craftsmanship First.

Flash Second.

Style without substance is just noise. We build cars that perform as incredibly as they look. Every weld, every stitch, and every line of code is executed with uncompromising precision.
